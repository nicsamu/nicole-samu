M.S. Degree Project

Thesis: "Spatial Discrepancies between NHDPlus and LIDAR-Derived Stream Networks" I statistically analyzed spatial discrepancies between NHDPlus and LIDAR-derived stream network datasets within and among three topographically different watersheds (8-digit HUCs) in North Carolina to provide a more in-depth empirical understanding of the differences between these types of datasets and their associated implications for water resource initiatives.

Completed: May 2012
