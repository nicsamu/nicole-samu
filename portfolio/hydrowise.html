HydroWISE

HydroWISE (Hydropower Water Infrastructure Information for Sustainable Energy) is a project providing a web portal for centralized access to data, tools, maps, publications, and other information on research and data projects from the Department of Energy’s Water Power Technologies Office. The project is a large collaborative effort developed by Oak Ridge National Laboratory (ORNL) and funded by the Department of Energy’s (DOE) Water Power Technologies Office. I coordinated initial branding, graphics, and various website interface components for the project.
