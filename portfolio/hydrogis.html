HydroGIS Viewer

HydroGIS Viewer is a data exploration tool originally released in 2013 to support U.S. hydropower market acceleration. Team members of Oak Ridge National Laboratory’s National Hydropower Assessment Program project have coordinated this effort. I’ve been working with Geospatial Scientist, Dr. Yaxing Wei, on the management and development of the HydroGIS Viewer web service and database since 2012.

Contribution: 2012 – Present

Platform: ArcGIS Server
